Health Check vs. Assessment

Understanding the difference allows you to select the right engagement for your current maturity level.

Technical Health Check

A deep-dive into a single technology (e.g., Firewall, Active Directory, Azure Tenant). We export your configuration and compare it line-by-line against the vendor's "Golden Standard" and CIS Benchmarks.

The Silent Threat: Configuration Drift

Network environments are alive. Admins make changes to fix urgent issues, often bypassing security protocols. Over time, these small changes create massive holes.

Cyber Insurance Compliance

Insurers are denying claims based on "failure to maintain." Our reports provide the evidence of due diligence required to keep your policy valid.

Discover "Shadow IT"

We frequently find leftover migration artifacts (e.g. old NBN router rules), unauthorized SaaS apps, and testing servers exposed to the internet.

Operational Hygiene

Clean up the clutter. Removing unused objects, old user accounts, and redundant rules improves performance and reduces attack surface.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Health Check process.

Will the scan cause downtime?
No. Our health checks are non-intrusive. We perform read-only configuration exports or use passive scanning methods that do not impact network performance or production availability.
Do I need to provide admin credentials?
For deep-dive configuration reviews (like M365 or AD), we prefer temporary Read-Only Admin access or to run scripts via a screen-share session with your team, ensuring you maintain control of credentials.
How is this different from a Penetration Test?
A Pen Test simulates an attack to find a way in. A Health Check reviews the settings of the system itself against best practices. A system can pass a Pen Test (no known exploits) but fail a Health Check (bad practice management settings) which could lead to issues later.
Can you fix the issues you find?
Yes. Unlike pure auditing firms, Outcomex is a Systems Integrator. We can quote for remediation engineering hours to apply the fixes, harden the systems, and re-test to confirm security.
How long does an assessment take?
A standard single-technology Health Check (e.g. Firewall) typically takes 3-5 days from data collection to final report delivery. Larger, multi-environment assessments (Cloud + AD + Network) may take 2-3 weeks.
What standards do you map against?
We map our findings against globally recognized frameworks including CIS Benchmarks, NIST Cybersecurity Framework, the ACSC Essential Eight, and vendor-specific Golden Standards (e.g. Microsoft Secure Score).
Is this a one-off or recurring service?
While many clients start with a one-off "Baseline" assessment, we highly recommend annual or bi-annual reviews to combat configuration drift. We offer subscription models for quarterly check-ups.
Do you use automated tools or manual review?
We use a hybrid approach. Automated tools gather data quickly and identify common flaws, while our senior engineers perform manual logic checks to find complex architectural issues that automated tools often miss.
What do we need to prepare?
Preparation is minimal. We require a 30-minute scoping call, provision of read-only credentials (or a screen-share session), and network diagrams if available. We handle the heavy lifting.
How is data confidentiality handled?
All data is handled strictly within Australia on our secure, ISO 27001 certified infrastructure. Configuration files and logs are sanitized and securely deleted 30 days after the engagement concludes.
