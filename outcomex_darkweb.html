What is Dark Web Monitoring as a Service?

The "Dark Web" is a hidden network of sites not indexed by traditional search engines, often used by cybercriminals to trade stolen data.

Dark Web Monitoring is a continuous surveillance service. We deploy intelligent agents and human analysts to scan underground marketplaces, private forums, and ransomware leak sites. We look specifically for your corporate assets—domains, email addresses, IP addresses, and executive names.

Continuous 24/7 Scanning

Intelligence Sources

  • Hacker Forums Discussions on exploits & targets
  • Credential Markets Databases of stolen usernames/passwords
  • Paste Sites Leaked source code and API keys

Why It's Critical for Australian Business

Reactive security is no longer enough. In an era of the Privacy Act reviews and SOCI Act requirements, you need to know if your data is exposed before a breach occurs.

Reduce Dwell Time

Cybercriminals often have access for months before deploying ransomware. Early detection of sold credentials allows you to reset access before the attack launches.

Regulatory Compliance

Demonstrate due diligence to regulators and stakeholders. Proactive monitoring helps mitigate penalties under the Australian Privacy Act by proving active risk management.

Supply Chain Visibility

It's not just your data—it's your vendors'. We monitor for third-party breaches that could expose your environment through trusted connections.
