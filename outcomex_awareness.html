Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about building a resilient security culture.

How often should we run phishing simulations?
We recommend monthly simulations. Consistency is key to building "muscle memory." Frequent, low-impact testing is more effective than annual, high-stress tests, ensuring staff stay alert to evolving scams like invoice fraud and smishing.
Is this suitable for small teams or just enterprises?
Our program is scalable. We work with Australian SMEs who need basic compliance (Essential 8) as well as large enterprises requiring complex, segmented training programs. The content is designed to be relevant for all users, regardless of company size.
Does this help with cyber insurance applications?
Yes. Most cyber insurance providers now require evidence of ongoing security awareness training. Our monthly reports provide the documented proof you need to demonstrate a proactive risk management stance to insurers.
What happens if an employee keeps failing the phishing tests?
Our approach is educational, not punitive. Users who click multiple times are automatically enrolled in a "Repeat Clicker" workflow, which provides additional, supportive training to help close their specific knowledge gaps without shaming them.
Do you offer training for Board members and Executives?
Yes. We offer specialized "Whaling" defence modules and face-to-face executive briefings. These sessions focus on the specific risks high-value targets face, such as deepfakes, BEC, and personal data exposure.
How does this integrate with our existing IT systems?
We support seamless integration with Microsoft 365, Azure AD, and Okta. This ensures user lists are automatically synced, so new hires are enrolled immediately and leavers are removed without manual admin work.
Will the simulated emails get blocked by our spam filters?
No. During onboarding, our team guides your IT department through the process of whitelisting (allow-listing) our simulation servers. This ensures training emails reach your users' inboxes reliably while keeping real threats out.
Can we customise the content for our specific industry?
Absolutely. We have libraries of industry-specific content for Healthcare, Finance, Government, Construction, and more. This ensures the scenarios (e.g., "Patient Data Update" vs "Site Inspection Schedule") feel relevant to your staff.
Is the training Australian-based?
Yes. Outcomex is Australian-owned and operated. Our content features local brands, Australian accent voiceovers, and scenarios relevant to the AUNZ threat landscape. All data is hosted onshore to meet data sovereignty requirements.
How long does onboarding take?
We aim for rapid time-to-value. For standard deployments, we can have your platform configured, whitelisted, and your first baseline simulation launched within 1-2 weeks of project kick-off.
